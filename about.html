Global Expertise & Local Experience

Welcome to OpexNetwork, the first TowerCo in Cameroon and Central Africa. OpexNetwork is specialized in the provision of wireless telecom passive infrastructure on a lease basis in Central Africa region. Expertise, quality, delivering performance to our clients, and much, much more.



  • Build To Suit

    Our Team offers customized Build-to-Suit programs (Turn-key) where satisfying the network requirements and deployment needs of operators comes first.

  • Colocation

    We make available to our customers space and services on an existing tower to a mobile service provider who needs point-to-point secure communication.

  • Buy & Lease-Back

    We buy network operator's towers and lease space, and provide services back to them. This frees up their capital and allow them to expand their network quickly. Plus, they know how getting best in class servicesand up to date practice.

  • Specific Solutions

    We provide our customer with temporary coverage solutions like mobile stations, or any temporary reinforcement of their subscribers reach. We also adapt our roll out to the environment to make it non agressive and pleasant to the landscape.
